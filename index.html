Hi, I’m AJ!

“Making data make sense—one line of code at a time.”

 I’m a data analyst and scientist who enjoys digging into messy data and finding interesting stories that hide inside. I’ve always been curious about how things work, and data gives me the perfect excuse to ask questions, explore patterns, and build some cool stuff.
 Originally from Fonda, NY and now living in Richmond, VA. Before moving to Richmond, I studied at the Rochester Institute of Technology where I received an Bachelors Degree in Economics ('13) and then a Masters in Business Administration ('14). Most recently, I completed a Masters in Data Science ('25) from Old Dominion University, where I concentrated in Business Intelligence Analytics.
 Currently I work as a Business Insights Lead for the Private Label team at McKesson Medical Surgical. I’ve worked on all kinds of projects—from building predictive models and automating reporting to creating interactive dashboards that make data easy to understand (and kind of fun to look at). I use tools like SQL, Python and PowerBI daily, and I’m always experimenting with new ways to make data more useful and accessible.
 When I’m not wrangling and exploring data, you’ll probably find me out and about with my wife and my two daughters enjoying the wide array of things Richmond has to offer. Always open to collaborating or chatting about all things data!
